# Unlisted entities

Adds 1 monster and 18 items to the game.

## Monsters
- **The Teapot**: a 6 legged teapot that throws boiling water at you.

## Items

### Weapons
- **Tranquilizer gun** (200$): Fires projectiles that can tranquilize targets.
- **Solid bat** (600$): A durable bat to hit mobs or players with.
- **Fragile bat** (50$): A one-use bat to hit mobs or players with.

### Consumables
- **Small O2 tank** (30$): Regenerates 30% oxygen.
- **Large O2 tank** (80$): Regenerates 80% oxygen.
- **Band-aid box** (20$): Regenerates 40% health.
- **Medkit** (50$): Regenerates 100% health.
- **Energy bar** (25$): Provides a temporary player boost.
- **Invisibility spray** (45$): Grants temporary invisibility.

### Explosives
- **Pop-it** (7$): A small explosive that explodes on impact.
- **Silver fulminate crystal** (150$): A powerful explosive crystal that explodes on impact.
- **Grenade** (30$): A standard explosive grenade.
- **Semtex grenade** (50$): A sticky grenade with a ticking timer.
- **Electric grenade** (90$): An explosive that releases an electric discharge. Maybe it can recharge your items ?

### Equipables
- **Froggy boots** (100$): Higher jumps.
- **Cursed Doll** (120$): A mysterious cursed doll. Maybe it can stimulate monster activity in the Old World ?
- **Angel wings** (100$): Wings that mitigate falling speed and stun.
- **Bright vest** (200$): A vest that provides light.
